ABOUT US

Khmer Organic Cooperative was established in 2014.

We are working along the whole agricultural supply chain from the farm gate to market places. Khmer Organic runs our businesses with a strong sense of responsibility and ethical values aligned with our corporate social responsibilities.

All our products and services are focused on creating real quality. We work in close cooperation with relevant smallholder farmers and other agricultural cooperatives.
