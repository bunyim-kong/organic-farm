About Us

Khmer Organic Cooperative is a social enterprise, working along the whole agricultural supply chain from farm gate to markets. In cooperation with all stakeholders in green sector, especially farmers in the rural areas of Cambodia, KOC brings fresh and high-quality organic produces to consumers and creates awareness of organic agriculture for better health, a better environment, and sustainable socio-economic development.

"Nature knows best. Organic farming listens to the land, grows with care, and helps heal the Earth."
